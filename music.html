Coolhand cal

I am 28 years old. Born and raised In Baltimore Maryland, Life was often chaotic growing up with 13 people in one house. Music was my escape and, in some ways, I feel like it helped raise me. I would Sit on my roof with my headphones on as my fav artists would perfectly capture what I was struggling with like shining a light in the dark. I admire the artist as much as I do the art. How they can step on stage grab the mic and speak my heart, or at least a part. So here I stand working to Finnish what they started.

"Started with nothing but sometimes nothing is a pretty cool hand."
